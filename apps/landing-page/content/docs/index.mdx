## 🧠 The Strategy: Institutional Logic

We don't gamble on volatility; we navigate it. Zap Pilot monitors the macro pulse of the market to keep your portfolio on the right side of the trend.

- **Macro Indicators:** We track the **200-Day Moving Average (200MA)** and **Fear & Greed Index (FGI)** to identify market regimes.
- **Dynamic Rebalancing:**
  - **Greed/Bull:** Capitalize on trends with **GMX BTC/ETH**.
  - **Fear/Bear:** Rotate to safety and liquidation yield via **Hyperliquid (HLP)**.
  - **Sideways:** Earn trading fees and accumulate BTC through **Morpho**.
- **ETH/BTC Rotation Overlay:** On top of the risk-on/off gate, we watch ETH/BTC relative strength versus its own 200-DMA. When ETH outperforms, the risk bucket tilts toward ETH; when BTC leads, we rotate back. A 30-day cooldown after each cross prevents whipsaw.

---

## ⚡ Execution: Intent-Based & One-Click

Each rebalance ships as one bundled intent, executed in your own wallet — **EIP-7702** batch on supported wallets, **multicall3** as a fallback. No custody hand-off, no approvals spread across days.

1.  **Monitor:** The engine tracks 200MA, FGI, and ETH/BTC relative strength 24/7.
2.  **Signal:** When a regime shifts, you get a Telegram message with the new target allocation (BTC / ETH / stable) and a pre-built transfer bundle ready to sign.
3.  **Execute:** Sign one **Batch Transaction**. One click to rebalance across multiple protocols and chains.
