Zap Pilot is BlackRock-style portfolio discipline running inside a wallet you already own. A rules engine watches objective market signals, prepares a rebalance bundle, and asks you to sign it from your own EOA. Buy in fear, defend in greed, no emotion, no custody.

## The three pillars

The portfolio is always a mix of three understandable assets — no tokens you can't pronounce, no protocols you don't understand:

- **S&P 500** via Ondo's tokenized index — the equity pillar.
- **BTC + ETH** — crypto risk-on, with an ETH/BTC relative-strength rotation overlay.
- **Stablecoins** — defensive cash leg for risk-off regimes.

Allocation across the three pillars shifts with the market regime.

## What stays in your wallet

<Callout type="info" title="Self-custody by construction">
  Your funds live in your own externally-owned account. Zap Pilot **never
  custodies, never holds keys, and never asks for standing approvals**. Each
  rebalance is a pre-built transaction bundle — you review, you sign, you move
  on.
</Callout>
