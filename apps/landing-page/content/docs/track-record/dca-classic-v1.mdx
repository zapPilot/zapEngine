## Overview

**Strategy ID:** `dca_classic`
**Version:** v1
**Start Date:** 2024-12-02
**Status:** Backtest only

DCA Classic is a pure mechanical baseline that invests a fixed dollar amount every week into a 60/40 BTC/ETH portfolio, with no regime awareness, no rebalancing, and no discretion.

## Rationale

DCA Classic exists as a fair comparison baseline. It captures the beta return of consistent crypto exposure without any active management decisions. If Zap Pilot cannot outperform DCA Classic on a risk-adjusted basis after costs, the strategy has failed its core thesis.

## Parameters

- **Weekly investment:** $100 (fixed)
- **Allocation:** 60% BTC, 40% ETH (static, never rebalanced)
- **Execution:** Market order, Monday 00:00 UTC
- **No stop-loss, no take-profit, no fee optimization**

## Performance Attribution

Zap Pilot's live performance is measured against DCA Classic across three dimensions:

1. **Total return** — cumulative growth of $1 invested at start
2. **Risk-adjusted return** — Sharpe / Sortino ratio comparison
3. **Drawdown protection** — max drawdown and time spent underwater
